About me
======
I am a researcher in Engineering with Artificial Intelligence (AI) for applications in Nonlinear Materials and Structural systems, currently a Senior Research Associate in Data Science for Materials Engineering at the University of Bristol (UK). My work sits at the intersection of nonlinear computational mechanics and dynamics, machine learning, and experimental testing, with the overarching goal of developing data-centric frameworks for multiscale modelling, design by analysis and digital twins of engineering structures under extreme operational conditions.
